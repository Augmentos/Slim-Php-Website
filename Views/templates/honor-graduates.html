Honorary Graduates

The Honorary Degree is one of higher education's most significant accolades and the University of Aberdeen awards them twice a year to individuals who have made a significant contribution in their field. The Honorary Degree is not only to acknowledge and celebrate the individuals' success but also to provide our current students with inspirational role models. A list of all of our honorary graduates is available to view on the main University of Aberdeen website.
