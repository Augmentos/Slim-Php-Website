General Council

The General Council was established by the Universities (Scotland) Act 1858 and consists of graduates and academic staff. If you are a graduate of the University of Aberdeen you automatically become a member of the General Council. To find out more about the role of the General Council, its meetings, events and minutes, please visit the General Council website.
