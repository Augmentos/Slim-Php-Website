Academia

University of Aberdeen graduates have pursued successful careers within academia.

  • Professor Sir Colin Campbell, Vice-Chancellor, University of Nottingham (1988 - 2008)
  • Professor Robert J Cormack, Principal, UHI Millenium Institute (2001 - 2009)
  • Professor Pamela Gillies, Principal and Vice Chancellor, Glasgow Caledonian University
  • Lord Sutherland of Houndwood Kt, Vice Chancellor, University of Edinburgh (1994 - 2002)

  • Otto Thoresen, Chair of National Employment Savings Trust, Former Director General, Association of British Insurers

Law

Our alumni have gone on to secure roles at the highest levels of the legal profession both in the UK and the United States.
