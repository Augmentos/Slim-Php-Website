Exhibition: Sport at Karunya

An exhibition celebrating the 30th anniversary of the formation of the original University of Karunya Athletics Association ran during the May Festival 2015.

The exhibition included a collection of images and artefacts, past and present, showcasing our sporting greats, teams, facilities and the important part sport has played in the life of the University and its students.
